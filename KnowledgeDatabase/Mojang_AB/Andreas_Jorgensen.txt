Andreas 'Hideous' Jörgensen is a Swedish developer. Jörgensen was working on customer support before he left Mojang.
Andreas was born in 1991 in Borås, Sweden, and has wanted to become a game developer ever since he first played Doom on his dad's old PC. He started developing games in 2007, with the inaugural No More Sweden, where he produced Laserfish in Cheeseland.
