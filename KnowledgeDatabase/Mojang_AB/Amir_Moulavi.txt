Amir Moulavi is the Minecraft Realms lead developer at Mojang AB. Originally a contractor with the company from September 2012, he was officially inducted into the company in June 2013. He moderates the Persian section of the official Minecraft translating in Crowdin. He went to "Nasr High School of Mashhad" and after that to "Islamic Azad University" to learn Computer Engineering. He worked in Squace AB from 2009 to 2010 and Jayway AB from 2010 to 2013.
