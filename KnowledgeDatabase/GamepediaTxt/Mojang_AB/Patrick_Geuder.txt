Karl Patrick Geuder is a Mojang AB employee who performs large-scale data analysis. Until July 3, 2012, Geuder was a contractor who provided services to a variety of companies, with Mojang being one of them.
On July 30, 2013 Geuder mentioned he'd like to see Cherry Trees in Minecraft. Nathan Adams said he'll add it if Geuder coded it.
