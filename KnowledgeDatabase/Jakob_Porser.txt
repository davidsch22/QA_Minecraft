Jakob 'JahKob' Porsér was a co-founder and game designer at Mojang until he left the company along with Notch and Carl Manneh on November 6th, 2014. Jakob is credited as lead designer on Scrolls, Mojang's second major project. Jakob has since stepped down as Lead Designer of the project to be replaced by Måns Olson.
