Magenta Dye is a tertiary Color made of combining one Purple Dye and a Pink Dye in a Crafting block.
Can be combined with Wool to make Magenta Wool Magenta Wool.
Can be applied to Sheep, which can yield 1-3 blocks of Magenta Wool when shorn.this is recommended,because it allows a player to get more than one dyed wool per dye.
